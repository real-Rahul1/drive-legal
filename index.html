ABOUT DRIVELEGAL

Empowering drivers with knowledge

DriveLegal was built for the National Road Safety Hackathon 2026, organised by CoERS, RBG Labs, IIT Madras. Our mission: eliminate ignorance of traffic laws as a cause of road accidents.

By providing location-specific, up-to-date information on traffic regulations, we help citizens understand their obligations — and the consequences of violations — before they get behind the wheel.
